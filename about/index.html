A little bit about me.

After many years working in education I decided it was time for a change and a new challenge. Starting my own business during the pandemic gave me a sneek peek into the world of wordpress and I became fascinated with the intricacies of how to design my own website and started digging a little deeper.

After investigating various boot camps I decided against this very intense approach to learning and stumbled across Skillcrush. I couldn't be happier with the classes, the friendly community and the encouragement from my likeminded skillcrushers who are embarking on this new journey.
